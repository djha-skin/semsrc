## Council Communication Guidelines

### When to Consult Council
- **Major architectural changes** affecting multiple components
- **Performance tradeoffs** with user experience impact
- **Security or data integrity** concerns
- **Git compatibility** decisions
- **Ontology and standards** choices
- **Migration strategy** changes

### How to Consult Council
1. **Prepare decision context** with clear options
2. **Use appropriate persona checklists** for review
3. **Schedule focused sessions** for complex decisions
4. **Document council feedback** and decisions
5. **Follow up with implementation** and results

### Council Decision Documentation
- **Decision ID**: Unique identifier for each decision
- **Context**: Background and alternatives considered
- **Council Input**: Summary of each persona's feedback
- **Decision**: Final choice and rationale
- **Implementation**: Action items and timeline
- **Review Date**: When to reassess the decision

## Risk Mitigation for Council Activation

### Risk: Council decisions slow down implementation
**Mitigation**: Time-box council reviews, use decision matrix for appropriate escalation

### Risk: Council feedback conflicts with implementation reality
**Mitigation**: Regular check-ins, prototype-driven feedback, iterative refinement

### Risk: Council becomes bottleneck for decisions
**Mitigation**: Clear decision authority matrix, delegation to implementation team for minor decisions

### Risk: Council feedback not implemented
**Mitigation**: Document council recommendations, track implementation status, regular review meetings
